Invited manuscript for the special issue of Journal of Phonetics, "Marking 50 Years of Research on Voice Onset Time and the Voicing Contrast in the Worldâ€™s Languages" (eds., T. Cho, G. Two types of analyses were performed:

2. Random Forests by Conditional Inference Trees as a classification technique [see notebook](Voicing and Gemination - Random Forests.nb.html)
